📖 The Origin Story

Born From The Group Chat.

The Random Store started where every good idea starts — in a group chat at 2am. We were sending memes back and forth, and someone said "yo this should be a shirt." So we made it a shirt.

Now we drop original meme clothing every Friday. No bootlegs, no stolen screenshots — every design is drawn from scratch by people who are extremely, irreversibly online.

Limited runs only. When a drop sells out, it's gone forever. That tee you're wearing? Nobody can buy it anymore. That's the point.
